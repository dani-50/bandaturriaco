On Saturday, June 13, at 8:30 PM, in the spaces adjacent to the municipal hall (in case of bad weather: at the PalaMarson),
the band of the Società Filarmonica di Turriaco will be the protagonist of a charity concert in support of the association
La Melagrana, a social cooperative that, through initiatives such as the recently opened restaurant-pizzeria «AUTstanding»
in Monfalcone, aims to restore dignity, autonomy and employment opportunities to people with an autism spectrum diagnosis.

Saturday's concert stems from the commitment that the Filarmonica di Turriaco has been carrying out for years towards
the local community, seeing music not only as artistic expression but as an instrument of social cohesion and civic responsibility.

For the evening, the Turriaco ensemble, conducted by maestro Fulvio Dose, will be joined — in a long-standing
collaboration — by the Tita Michelas band from Fiumicello, whose maestro Gabriele Zimolo will alternate with
m. Dose in conducting the pieces on the programme.

The evening's repertoire will be entirely dedicated to British music, ranging from traditional island folk tunes to
classical pieces and pop music arrangements.

The event is part of the eighteenth edition of *Note di Costituzione*, the festival that since April 10 has
animated the area with a rich calendar of events involving local associations, and will be the occasion for the
presentation of the new website with which the Società Filarmonica showcases its activities and history.

Admission is by free donation. The community is invited to attend and make a concrete contribution to those who believe
that inclusion through work is the truest path to autonomy and self-respect.
